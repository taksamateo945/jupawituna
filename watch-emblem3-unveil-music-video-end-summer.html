WATCH: Emblem3 unveil music video for &quot;End Of The Summer&quot;

Publish date: 2024-06-10

Emblem3 have unveiled the music video for their new single.

In 2012, Wesley and Keaton Stromberg teamed up with their friend Drew Chadwick to audition on The X Factor, and after some success, they released their debut album “Nothing To Lose” with Syco Music and Columbia Records.

Unfortunately, the trio went their separate ways in 2014 but to the delight of fans they reunited a while ago, at the beginning of 2016 and now they’re unstoppable. It seems like they want to steal your heart this summer.

They launched their very own application in February, and they still have lots of things under their belts.

Now that the incredible Emblem 3 returned to their music they announced upcoming projects, new album, and now they finally brought the new music video for their song “End Of The Summer”.
