Brooke Shields: 25 Things You Don't Know About Me

Publish date: 2024-03-25

Army Wives' Brooke Shields, 48, spills her secrets to Us. Her new movie, The Hot Flashes, is out in July.

1. I get shots because I'm allergic to my dog.

2. My first movie was Annie Hall but my scenes were cut.

3. I love curling up in the Allegra La-Z-Boy chair to read while my daughters (Rowan, 10, and Grier, 7) do their homework.

4. I always regret not continuing to study piano.

5. I put on '40s music and dance alone in my house.

6. I go to the Container Store when I'm stressed.

7. I have two tattoos.

8. I am afraid to skydive.

9. Laughing is my favorite thing to do.

10. When I was a kid, I had a Siberian husky. When she died, my mom told me that she went to Alaska to pull sleds.

11. I can tie a cherry stem into a knot with my tongue.

12. I can open a twist-off beer bottle with my forearm.

13. The first day filming Army Wives, I fell down the stairs of the C-17 plane I was supposed to have been flying.

14. I did an internship at the San Diego Zoo in high school.

15. I knocked down a support beam of my house pulling the car into the garage when my heel got stuck.

16. I can still zip up my original Calvin Klein jeans.

17. I graduated with honors in French literature from Princeton University.

18. My exercise of choice is spinning at SoulCycle.

19. My parents got divorced when I was 5 months old.

20. I am addicted to Ice Breakers Sours.

21. I was born two months premature. I weighed 5 pounds, 3 ounces (what happened?).

22. I hate "to be continued" on TV shows.
