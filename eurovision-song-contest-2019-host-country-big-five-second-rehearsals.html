Eurovision Song Contest 2019: Host Country &amp; Big Five Second Rehearsals

Publish date: 2024-04-07

Can you believe that Eurovision Week is here? It’s all come around so quickly. Behind-the-scenes of the contest, rehearsals are in full swing. We’ve already seen the first rehearsals from the semi-finalists, the first rehearsals from the host country and the big five; we’ve also seen the second rehearsals from the semi-finalists. So it’s now time for the host country and the big five to take to the stage for their second rehearsals.

Last year, Netta won the contest with her song “Toy”, bringing the Eurovision Song Contest to Israel. It will be held at the Expo Tel Aviv, Tel Aviv; with the semi-finals taking place on 14 May and 16 May, whilst the Grand Final takes place on 18 May 2019.
