Demi Lovato Joins The Lineup For Billboard's Hot 100 Fest

Publish date: 2024-03-19

Demi Lovato is set to perform as a special guest at the annual Billboard Hot 100 Fest. She will be joining many other big name artists who are also performing at the festival which takes place at the Northwell Health at Jones Beach Theater in Long Island, New York, on August 19th and 20th.

In addition to performing, Lovato will also hang out with the winner of Billboard’s Hot 100 contest, sponsored by Herbal Essences. The grand prize, for one lucky fan, includes not only a meet and greet with Demi Lovato, but also airfare and backstage festival access. Prizes will also be given to many other additional winners.

As we said before, Lovato is performing among many other big name acts. The headliners of the two-day festival include, Big Sean, Major Lazer, Zedd, Demi Lovato, Bebe Rexha, Camila Cabello, DJ Khaled, Echosmith, and Gucci Mane.

Lovato has had many hit singles over the years. Songs like “Confident”, “Cool For The Summer”, “Stone Cold”, and “Heart Attack” are just a few names of the songs fans can expect to hear from her once she hits the stage. Demi Lovato has also had major Billboard chart success with her Cheat Codes collab, “No Promises”. With her newly released songs and past hits, we can definitely expect her to give New York an explosive live performance.

Recently, she also told Billboard that she will “release a soulful, R&B-tinged new album (the follow-up to 2015’s Confident) later this year”. We can only hope she teases a new song off of her new album at the Hot 100 Fest.

The addition of Demi Lovato to the festival lineup is not the only announcement Billboard has in store. The popular music company is telling fans to stay tuned for more announcements to come.
