Tusky Valley Bus Accident: Katelyn Owens John Mosely Four others died

Publish date: 2024-04-04

A charter bus carrying students from Tusky Valley was tragically rear-ended by a semi-truck on an Ohio highway. Six people, including three students, lost their lives in the devastating accident.

What happened? Victims of the Accident

Among the victims were Katelyn Owens and John W. Mosely, both 18 years old, who were identified as two of the three students who tragically died on the scene. The names of the other four victims, including one student and three chaperones, have not been released at this time.

Community Reaction

The Tuscarawas Valley School District, deeply affected by the tragedy, said it had the “wind knocked out of it” by the sudden loss of six of its members. The community has since come together to mourn the tragic loss, with a gathering held to remember the victims. The school district superintendent confirmed the deaths and expressed deep sorrow over the incident.

Tusky Valley Bus Accident: Ongoing Investigation

Authorities are currently investigating the circumstances surrounding the crash. The accident took place on Interstate 70, near the state Route 310 interchange, in Licking County. As the investigation unfolds, many are left grappling with the tragic loss and seeking answers.
