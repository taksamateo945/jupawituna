Demi Lovato Looks Exquisite In The Versace Jungle Print That J.Lo Made Famous! See The Photos!

Publish date: 2024-04-14

Demi Lovato looks exquisite in the Versace Jungle print that Jennifer Lopez made famous. Jennifer became a fashion icon when she wore the plunging Versace dress to the 42nd Grammy Awards that were held on February 23, 2000. Donatella Versace designed the dress and Jennifer wore it again — twenty years later! Appearing on NBC’s SNL, Jennifer dazzled in the dress and Versace celebrated the print’s twenty-year anniversary with a new campaign and new designs. Jennifer teamed up with Kendall Jenner for a sensational fashion shoot and demand for Versace’s jungle print has been on the rise!

You may see a video interview with Jennifer Lopez as she discusses the iconic Verace jungle dress that she wore in 2000 and people’s reaction to the look below.

Jennifer was part of the dress’s relaunch and Donatella Versace shared plenty of photos featuring Jennifer and Kendall Jenner wearing the jungle print on her official Instagram account. On Jennifer Lopez’s birthday, Donatella shared a photo of the two with Jennifer wearing the print.

Demi Lovato posted two photos of herself wearing the modernized, Versace jungle print pantsuit and fans loved the look on her.

Demi wore the Versace Jungle print leggings paired with the Palm Print bra top and the Jungle print, single-breasted blazer. The colors looked fabulous on Demi and she posed outside in the outfit giving fans two views of the look. In one photo, she looked straight at the camera and showed off the outfit from the front. In the second photo, Demi turned to the side and looked over her shoulder giving a sideways glance at the camera.

For footwear, Demi chose the Nudist sandals in the color neon by Stuart Weitzman. The shoes retail for approximately $450. You may see the photos that Demi Lovato shared with her 92.7 million Instagram followers.
