Lauren Conrad Admits She's &quot;Pretty Basic,&quot; Covers Cosmopolitan

Publish date: 2024-06-18

We can’t believe our eyes! After a memorable feud with Allure for categorizing her as “basic” in its July 2014 issue, Lauren Conrad is now owning it.

The Hills alum, 29, famously took to Twitter last year after hearing of Allure‘s categorization, saying, “I definitely just got called a basic b—ch! Haha! Really @Allure_magazine?”

But this year Conrad, who married William Tell in September of last year, has a different outlook concerning the colloquial phrase. “I’m probably pretty basic,” she told Cosmo. “But I’m also a pretty happy person, so that’s okay with me.”
