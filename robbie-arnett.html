Robbie Arnett: The Milo Greene Singer Married To Elizabeth Olsen

Publish date: 2024-05-11

Robbie Arnett, the lead vocalist of the indie band Milo Greene, is Elizabeth Olsen’s husband. He is also a singer-songwriter.

The couple first met in 2017 and got engaged in 2019.

In 2021, they tied the knot in a private ceremony. They welcomed their first child, a son, in the same year.

Who Is Robbie Arnett?

Arnett, 31, hails from Los Angeles and works as a musician in the band Milo Greene. The trio unveiled their self-titled debut album in 2012.

Subsequently, they released Control in 2015 and their third album, Adult Contemporary, in 2018.

Olsen expresses her admiration for her musician boyfriend, mentioning during a 2017 interview on Sway in the Morning that she loves the fact that he is part of a cool band.

He’s Part Of Milo Green Band

Robbie, as it turns out, is a member of the Los Angeles-based band Milo Greene. The trio of singer-songwriters released their self-titled debut album in 2012.

They followed it up with their 2015 project Control and released Adult Contemporary in 2018.

Wondering about the origin of the group’s name? Let us shed some light on that with insights straight from Robbie himself.

In 2012, Robbie told NPR, “We created a Gmail and started sending emails and making calls on behalf of Milo Greene, in search of a booking agent and manager.”

Their efforts quickly paid off as they secured numerous gigs. Eventually, Milo Greene became their official band name in real life. Now you know the story behind it!

Robbie Arnett and Olsen Are super low-key about their marriage

Robbie and Elizabeth got married a year before their marriage was officially confirmed.

In an interview with SiriusXM’s The Jess Cagle Show, Elizabeth disclosed that their wedding happened before the pandemic.

They opted for an elopement followed by a separate wedding ceremony.

The timing proved advantageous because Elizabeth had to work in England. If they hadn’t been married already, logistical challenges could have arisen.

Visa complications would have prevented Robbie from joining her. In short, their fortunate timing saved them from potential difficulties.

Elizabeth’s husband is into philanthropy

In 2018, Robbie joined Elizabeth as they collaborated with the nonprofit organization, The Latitude Project, to host a trip to Nicaragua.

The purpose of the trip was to build fresh water remedies alongside locals in the aftermath of Hurricane Nate.

Robbie Arnett is a published author

Robbie and Elizabeth teamed up to author a book titled “Hattie Harmony: Worry Detective.”

They published the book in June 2022, providing children with effective techniques to manage anxiety.

Penguin Random House portrays it as a distinctive addition to the wellness genre, forming part of a series.

Excitingly, a sequel to the book is scheduled for release this summer.

How did Olsen and Robbie Arnett meet?

They met in Mexico through a mutual friend during their vacation and began dating in February 2017.

According to a source who spoke to E! News, the pair were in an “exclusive relationship” at the time, and the actress was genuinely “excited” about her new partner.

The source added that although their relationship was still new, they seemed to have a strong liking for each other.

In July of the same year, they were spotted together for the first time at a public event attending the Christian Dior show during Paris Fashion Week.

Who Is Elizabeth Olsen?

Elizabeth Olsen, an American actress, was born on February 16, 1989, in Sherman Oaks, California.

She has gained recognition as the younger sister of Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen, who became famous as child stars.

In her early teens, Olsen embarked on her acting career by appearing in movies alongside her sisters.
