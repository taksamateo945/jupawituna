Cole Sprouse And Lili Reinhart Reportedly No Longer An Item!

Publish date: 2024-07-25

Riverdale co-stars Lili Reinhart and Cole Sprouse are reportedly no longer an item! Sources have dished about their split via People and Page Six but they are still to confirm it personally.

This is not the first time that they spark breakup rumors, however!

The same happened last summer as well but at the time, the actor couple addressed the speculations in their cover story interview for W Magazine.

‘Don’t believe everything you read on the internet, kids,’ Reinhart told fans.

Furthermore, she also labeled Sprouse as her ‘boyfriend’ during another interview, this time for the September issue of Coveteur.

Then, in April, Cole was linked romantically to another girl, model Kaia Gerber, something that he was quick to deny, calling people out for their ‘baseless accusations.’

Around that same time, one insider dished via E! News that they were still dating but just quarantining separately.

‘Their relationship is always up and down, but they do talk every day and care very much about one another.’

The two have always kept their romance under wraps so it’s hard to know what really goes on between them.

Last year in May, Reinhart shared with W Magazine that it was Cole’s idea to be private and while she did not want that at first, she grew to appreciate it.
