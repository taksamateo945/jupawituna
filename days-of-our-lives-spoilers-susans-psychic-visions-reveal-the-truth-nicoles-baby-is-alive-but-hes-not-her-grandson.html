Days Of Our Lives Spoilers: Susans Psychic Visions Reveal The Truth

Publish date: 2024-02-29

Days of Our Lives spoilers and rumors tease Susan Banks’ (Stacy Haiduk) psychic visions may reveal the truth-that Nicole DiMera’s (Arianne Zucker) baby is alive.

But they could also reveal that Baby Boy DiMera (uncast) is not her grandson and so EJ DiMera (Dan Feurreriegel) is not the father!

Days Of Our Lives Spoilers: The Baby Died?

Nicole gave birth at the side of a road after a car crash with Dimitri Von Leuschner (Peter Porte) and Leo Stark (Greg Rikaart) who were trying to leave town.

Dimitri was speeding, trying to make it to meet Dr. Wilhelm Rolf (Richard Wharton) and his submarine, who his mother, Megan DiMera (Miranda Wilson) had sent.

Leo and Dimitri stopped and helped, Leo delivering the baby since Nicole’s water had broken-Dimitri took the preemie to the hospital but soon left, it was crawling with cops.

Dimitri took the baby to Sloan Petersen-Brady’s (Jessica Serfaty) apartment, since he found out Leo needed a lawyer-he’d been apprehended.

There, Melinda Trask (Tina Huang) took over, and a shady doctor she knew, Dr. Pierce (Steve Mize) soon went to the hospital and told EJ and Nicole the baby died!

DOOL Spoilers: Melinda Trask Has Convinced Sloan Petersen-Brady

Since she knows the baby is Nicole and Eric Brady’s, (Greg Vaughan) not EJ and Nicole’s, Melinda convinces Sloan to keep the baby-for her and Eric. She finally agrees, and soon Eric and Sloan are happy parents of a newborn, albeit a little premature.

Melinda had Dr. Pierce check the baby and from what he could tell, the baby was fine, although all he had was his doctor’s bag and no special equipment.

Blood tests and imaging should have been done on a preemie, but with this baby they were not-and he didn’t get his mother’s first milk which helps with immunity. That’s understandable, Nicole declining to nurse with two men there, albeit gay!

Days Of Our Lives Spoilers: Susan Banks Comes To Support Her Grieving Son

Soon, EJ or perhaps Johnny DiMera (Carson Boatman) calls Susan to let her know what happened, and she comes to Salem to support her grieving son EJ.

But she soon gets psychic visions when she comes near Nicole-her visions tell her the baby is alive, but also that EJ’s not his father! That part doesn’t make sense to her but she leaves it out when she tells EJ and Nicole she senses the baby is alive and in Salem.

It may not be an immediate solution, but it does prompt EJ to have this Dr. Pierce checked out—and he finds he has a criminal record. Eventually, the baby will be found and the truth will come out-but Susan’s not receiving his location!
